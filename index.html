Why We Started

We’ve been there – rushing between classes, trying to find something quick to eat, and ending up with bland or unhealthy food options. It’s frustrating, unsatisfying, and not how we want to fuel our days. That’s why we decided to make a change, for ourselves and for every student out there. Our mission is simple: to provide you with meals that are delicious, nutritious, and easy to get. We’re partnering with a trusted local Indian restaurant to bring you meals that not only taste great but also fit within your budget. Welcome to a better way to eat on campus.
